About Us

Riverview Volunteer Fire Department is dedicated to protecting the lives and property of our community in York County, South Carolina. Our team of dedicated volunteer firefighters and emergency responders are available 24/7 to answer the call when our neighbors need us most.

Founded on the principles of service, courage, and community, we combine professional training with local knowledge to provide the highest level of emergency response. Whether it's fire suppression, emergency medical services, or community education, Riverview VFD stands ready to serve.

Our Services

🔥

Fire Suppression

Rapid response to structure fires, vehicle fires, and wildland fires with state-of-the-art equipment and trained personnel.

🚑

Emergency Medical

First responder medical services providing critical care until advanced medical units arrive on scene.

⚠️

Rescue Operations

Vehicle extrication, technical rescue, and emergency assistance for a wide range of situations.

🏫

Fire Prevention

Community education programs, school visits, and fire safety inspections to prevent emergencies before they happen.

💧

Water Rescue

Specialized training and equipment for water-related emergencies in our local waterways.

🌪️

Disaster Response

Emergency response coordination during severe weather, natural disasters, and large-scale incidents.
