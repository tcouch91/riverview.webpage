About Us

Riverview Fire Department is dedicated to protecting the lives and property of our community in York County, South Carolina. Our team of dedicated firefighters and emergency responders are available 24/7 to answer the call when our neighbors need us most.

Founded on the principles of service, courage, and community, we combine professional training with local knowledge to provide the highest level of emergency response. Whether it's fire suppression, emergency medical services, or community education, Riverview FD stands ready to serve.
