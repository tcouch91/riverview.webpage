Station 2

Engine 2

Ladder 1

Squad 2

Station 2, our second station located at 141 Grant Farm Dr, extends our coverage and enhances our response capabilities. This modern facility is home to key pieces of apparatus, including Engine 2, a versatile pumper, and Ladder 1, our aerial ladder truck.

Ladder 1 is essential for fires in multi-story buildings, providing elevated water streams and a safe platform for rooftop operations and rescues. Station 2 also houses Squad 2, a quick-response support vehicle. Together, the crews and apparatus at Station 2 are vital to protecting the growing communities in our service area, particularly in dense residential neighborhoods like Baxter Village.
